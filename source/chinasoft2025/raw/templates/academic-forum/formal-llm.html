形式化方法与大模型技术

学术论坛 R27

一、论坛介绍

形式化方法在传统领域如安全关键任务软件工程、嵌入式系统、网络安全等领域中已有广泛应用，为确保系统正确性和可靠性提供了数学上的严格保障。当下，大模型的蓬勃发展为形式化方法提供了新的机遇。一方面，大模型强大的文本处理、计算和推理能力能够有效提升形式化建模和验证的效率，推动形式化方法在更多领域的广泛应用，为复杂系统的开发和验证提供有力支持；另一方面，形式化方法通过严格的数理逻辑方法，有望为大模型的安全性、可靠性、可解释性和可信性提供更高级别的安全保障，确保大模型在金融、医疗、自动驾驶、系统开发等高安全要求领域的可靠落地，从而促进大模型技术及产业的健康、安全、可持续发展。

本论坛旨在推动形式化方法与大模型的深度融合，催生新型验证理论，为企业提供更加可落地的验证工具与方法论；同时，推动形式化方法在大模型及其应用可信保障上的系统应用，助力构建社会公众对人工智能的信任，支撑数字经济的可持续发展。

四、论坛报告嘉宾简介

1. 报告人：Shing-Chi Cheung 教授

题目：Abstraction is a key: Incorporating and Evaluating LLMs on Natural Language Requirements to Verifiable Formal Proofs

摘要：随着 DeepSeek-R1 的流行与 AI4Math 研究的深入，大模型在辅助形式化证明写作方面的需求日益增长。作为数学推理最直接的应用场景，形式化推理与验证也获得持续关注。然而，近期的形式化推理大模型大多只针对单一形式化语言模型，缺乏对多形式化语言、多形式化任务场景的深度探索。 为了探索这个问题，我们首先对形式化验证任务进行了分层拆解，从非形式化的自然语言输入到可验证的形式化证明或可检测的模型；将传统的端到端形式化验证流程细化为六个子任务，包括验证需求分解、形式化规约片段生成、规约补全、填空，以及代码到形式化规约的自动生成。此外，我们进一步探索了形式化数据微调对大模型数学、推理和编程等任务上的迁移能力。通过对比微调前后在上述任务上的表现差异，以验证大模型能否通过形式化验证能力训练中习得推理、数学等元能力。最后，报告还将介绍对程序行为建模的初步探索。

简介：Professor Shing-chi Cheung from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology specializes in leveraging advanced testing methodologies, artificial intelligence technologies, and empirical research techniques to identify, diagnose, and repair faults in reliable and intelligent software systems. He holds the prestigious titles of lEEE Fellow and Chair Professor. He is supervising 14 doctoral students. Professor Cheung's contributions to software engineering are well-recognized, with papers widely presented at leading academic conferences and six ACM SIGSOF'T Outstanding Paper Awards in the last decade. He ranks among the top ten scholars in terms of publications at the premier conferences ICSE and ESEC/FSE. In 1998, Professor Cheung introduced Metamorphic Testing, which has since emerged as a leading testing methodology in artificial intelligence system testing. Recently, with the advancement and successful deployment of large language models in software testing, Professor Cheung has also made significant contributions to the field of intelligent software testing.

2. 报告人：孙军 教授

题目：Formal Methods for AI Agent Safety

摘要：AI agents are rapidly being deployed across a wide range of domains, including safety-critical applications. Unlike static LLMs, these agents can directly interact with and modify the real world—making AI safety not just a theoretical concern but an immediate and tangible one. However, unlike traditional software systems, AI agents operate through dynamically generated workflows, rendering conventional formal methods based on fixed execution paths insufficient. This raises a pressing question: if safety must be assured, how can we achieve it? In this talk, I will present our recent efforts toward ensuring the safety of AI agents and discuss several fundamental challenges that lie ahead.

简介：Jun Sun is a Professor at Singapore Management University. He earned his Bachelor’s and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from the National University of Singapore in 2002 and 2006, respectively, and has been a faculty member since 2010. Professor Sun’s research interests span formal methods, AI safety, and software engineering. He is passionate about designing algorithms to solve challenging real-world problems and is equally devoted to enjoying life. He has published extensively in top venues. For more information, please visit his website: https://sunjun.site.

4. 报告人：张民 教授

题目：Linking Symbolic Reasoning and Neural Computing via Abstraction

摘要：This talk focuses on bridging symbolic reasoning and neural computing — two core paradigms in AI — via abstraction. It first notes their inherent trade-offs: symbolic reasoning excels at interpretable logical deduction but struggles with noisy real-world data, while neural computing handles complex data well yet lacks transparency and abstract reasoning. The key challenge of integrating them is then highlighted, with abstraction introduced as the critical solution. The talk explains abstraction’s dual roles: it enhances symbolic reasoning by simplifying knowledge representation for flexible deduction, and enables neural computing to learn high-level abstract features beyond raw data patterns. Specific integration mechanisms (e.g., neuro-symbolic machines) are outlined, where abstraction converts symbolic rules into neural-processable vectors and translates neural-learned patterns into interpretable symbols.

简介：张民是华东师范大学软件工程学院的教授，上海市高可信计算重点实验室主任，同时也是中国计算机学会形式化方法专委会副主任。他于 2011 年在日本北陆先端科学技术大学院大学获得博士学位，2014 年回国加入华东师范大学。他的研究兴趣主要集中在安全关键系统的形式化方法上，包括实时嵌入式系统和人工智能驱动的系统。最近，他专注于智能系统的形式化验证以及基于形式化验证的可信智能控制系统训练与开发。

5. 报告人：孙猛 教授

题目：Automata-Based Steering of Large Language Models for Diverse Structured Generation

摘要：Large language models (LLMs) are increasingly tasked with generating structured outputs. While structured generation methods ensure validity, they often lack output diversity, a critical limitation that we confirm in our preliminary study. We propose a novel method to enhance diversity in automaton-based structured generation. Our approach utilizes automata traversal history to steer LLMs towards novel structural patterns. Evaluations show our method significantly improves structural and content diversity while maintaining comparable generation efficiency. Furthermore, we conduct a case study showcasing the effectiveness of our method in generating diverse test cases for testing open-source libraries.

简介：孙猛，北京大学数学科学学院信息与计算科学系教授，博士生导师， CCF形式化方法专委执行委员，CCF区块链专委执行委员，CSIAM区块链专委常务委员，CSIAM金融科技与算法专委常务委员，CAAI人工智能逻辑专委委员。主要研究领域包括程序理论、软件形式化方法、信息物理系统、深度学习、区块链与智能合约，主持及作为主要成员参与国家自然科学基金、重点研发计划等国家及省部级项目十余项，在TSE、TDSC、ICSE、FSE、NeuIPS、ICML、AAAI、FM等期刊及会议发表论文150余篇，获FSE2025、SBMF2017、TASE2015、AMAST2004等国际会议最佳/杰出论文奖，曾任ICFEM2024、FACS2024、TASE2023、ICFEM2018、TTSS2011、FACS2009等国际会议程序委员会主席及FM、TACAS等多个国际会议程序委员会委员。

6. 报告人：李昊坤 华为主任工程师 形式化方法专家

题目：大规模代码自动验证探索：潜在错误引导的规约生成框架

摘要：在形式化方法的发展中，如何实现大规模程序的自动化验证始终是关键挑战。传统演绎验证依赖人工编写的形式规约，这不仅是工业级应用中的主要成本来源，也成为限制验证规模扩展的核心瓶颈。近年来，大语言模型（LLM）为规约自动生成带来了新的契机，但仍受到上下文长度限制与跨过程语义推理困难等问题的制约。

简介：李昊坤，2022年获北京大学博士学位，现于华为2012可信费马实验室形式化方法专家，参与多个重要的形式化验证项目。主要研究方向为符号计算，定理证明及程序验证。
